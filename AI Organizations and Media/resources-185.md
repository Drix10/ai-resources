### 🤖 Robotics - Open-Source MolmoAct 2 Release

This article announces the release of MolmoAct 2, an open-source robotics model. It details the specific components made available to the public, aiming to advance research in embodied AI.

Key Points:

• MolmoAct 2 is an open-source robotics model, differing from common closed systems.

• Model weights are released for broad accessibility and research.

• An updated VLA architecture enhances the model's capabilities.

• A fully open action tokenizer is provided for customization.

• The MolmoAct 2-Bimanual YAM dataset, with over 700 hours of tabletop manipulation, is included.
