### 🤖 Distributed Systems - Global Time Synchronization

This article explores the complexities of managing time zones and ensuring accurate time synchronization in distributed computing environments. It addresses the challenges posed by geographical distribution and varying local regulations.

Key Points:

• Ensures data consistency across globally distributed services.

• Prevents discrepancies in logging, scheduling, and event timestamps.

• Facilitates accurate user experience for international applications.

• Supports compliance with regional data processing regulations.

🚀 Implementation:
1. Standardize Time Reference: Employ Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) for all internal system operations.
2. Implement Synchronization Protocols: Utilize NTP or similar protocols to maintain consistent server clocks.
3. Manage User Time Zones: Convert UTC to local time only at the presentation layer for display.
4. Handle Time Zone Changes: Incorporate robust libraries for accurate historical and future time zone transitions.
