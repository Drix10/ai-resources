### 🤖 AI in Education - Assessment Challenges

This post discusses how AI tools influence academic assessment strategies. It highlights the challenges educators face in maintaining academic integrity with the rise of AI-assisted student work.

Key Points:

• AI tools challenge traditional take-home assignments due to advanced capabilities.

• Shifting to in-class, closed-note assessments is a current mitigation strategy.

• Emerging AI wearables suggest future workarounds to even in-class controls.

• In-class tests often lack the complexity or depth of take-home assignments.
