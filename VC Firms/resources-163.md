Key Points:

• Excessive equity retained by departed co-founders can deter new investors.

• Equity held by pre-pivot investors may become misaligned with new company direction.

• Early investors acquiring too much ownership can limit future financing flexibility.

• Proactive equity management is crucial for long-term startup health and fundraising.


🚀 Implementation:
