### 🤖 Energy Infrastructure - Grid-Scale Storage Challenges

This article analyzes India's escalating electricity demand and its implications for renewable energy integration. It highlights the critical need for grid-scale battery storage to support solar power expansion.

Key Points:

• India's electricity demand will double by 2030.

• India added 18GW of solar power in FY2024, a record.

• The grid requires substantial storage for intermittent renewable power.

• Current battery storage is 3GWh, needing 100GWh by 2030.
