‚Ä¢ Security and content filtering are best implemented at the deployment level, allowing for granular control and easier updates.


‚Ä¢ Integrating safety measures at the host level allows for consistent application across multiple models and easier auditing.

‚Ä¢  A host-level approach provides better scalability and maintainability for large-scale deployments.
