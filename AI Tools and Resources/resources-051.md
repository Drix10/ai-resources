### ü§ñ Machine Learning - Popular Algorithms

This article provides a brief overview of several popular machine learning algorithms.  It aims to offer a concise summary of their key characteristics.


Key Points:

‚Ä¢ Linear Regression:  Predicts a continuous target variable based on a linear relationship with predictor variables.


‚Ä¢ Logistic Regression: Predicts the probability of a categorical dependent variable.


‚Ä¢ Support Vector Machines (SVM):  Finds an optimal hyperplane to maximize the margin between different classes.


‚Ä¢ Decision Trees:  Builds a tree-like model to classify or regress data based on a series of decisions.


‚Ä¢ Random Forests:  An ensemble method that combines multiple decision trees to improve prediction accuracy and robustness.


‚Ä¢ Naive Bayes:  A probabilistic classifier based on Bayes' theorem with strong (naive) independence assumptions between features.


‚Ä¢ K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN):  Classifies data points based on the majority class among its k nearest neighbors.


‚Ä¢ K-Means Clustering:  Partitions data into k clusters based on similarity.
