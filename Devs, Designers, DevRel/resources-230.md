### 🤖 Display Technology - CRT Emulation with Modern Displays

This article discusses the current ability of shaders and filters to replicate the visual characteristics of Cathode Ray Tube (CRT) displays on modern OLED screens. It considers whether this emulation can achieve visual equivalence for most observers.

Key Points:

• Shaders and digital filters are used to simulate visual artifacts specific to CRTs.

• Modern OLED displays offer high contrast ratios suitable for emulating CRT visuals.

• The goal is to reproduce aspects like scanlines and phosphor glow through software.

• A key question is the perceptual similarity between a filtered OLED and a true CRT.
