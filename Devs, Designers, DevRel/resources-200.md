### 💡 Local AI - Practical VRAM Heuristics

This article addresses the primary challenge of VRAM requirements when setting up local AI environments. It provides practical heuristics for selecting appropriate models based on available GPU VRAM.

Key Points:

• VRAM capacity is the critical constraint for running AI models locally

• Higher VRAM enables larger models and extended context windows

• Specific model sizes and quantization levels correspond to VRAM tiers

• Efficient VRAM management allows for powerful local AI inference


🚀 Implementation:
1. For 24GB VRAM: Run 27B Q4_K_M models with a 262k context.
2. For 16GB VRAM: Utilize 13B Q5_K_M models at 32k or 9B Q8_0 at 64k.
3. For 12GB VRAM: Target 8B Q5_K_M models suitable for 16k context.
4. For 8GB VRAM: Deploy 4B Q4_K_M models with an 8k context window.
