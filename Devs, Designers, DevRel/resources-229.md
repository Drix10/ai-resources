### 🤖 Game Development - Quake to Roblox Port

This article introduces Robloquake, a direct function-by-function port of Quake and Quakeworld, including their mods, to the Roblox platform. The project is implemented in Luau and is available as open-source code.

Key Points:
• Robloquake is a function-by-function port of Quake and Quakeworld.

• The port operates within Roblox using Luau.

• It supports original Quake mods.

• The project is open source and playable.

### 🤖 Robotics Runtime - Dexmal DW05 Release

Dexmal has released DW05-Base and DW05-Robotwin, new components for the Dexbotic runtime. These releases introduce advanced capabilities for robotic control and AI integration.

Key Points:
• DW05-Base and DW05-Robotwin are new Dexbotic runtime components.

• They provide a 32D action and proprioception interface.

• The releases include a bundled VAE, text encoder, and tokenizer.

• Future updates plan for robot-action prediction and robot-view video rollout.
