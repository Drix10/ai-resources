### 🤖 Product Development - AI Era Changes

This article examines how artificial intelligence capabilities are reshaping established product development processes. It highlights a fundamental shift in the assumptions underlying product creation.

Key Points:
• AI changes the fundamental assumption of high building costs in product development.

• Traditional upfront de-risking through extensive specs and research is less critical.

• Product teams can adapt strategies to current development economics.

### 🤖 AI Business Strategy - Horizontal Integration

This article analyzes the strategic choices of prominent AI companies regarding their integration models. It discusses how their approach to capital expenditure and infrastructure has influenced their success.

Key Points:
• OpenAI and Anthropic avoided vertical integration in their core strategy.

• Cloud providers absorb significant capital expenditure for AI infrastructure.

• This strategy provides an advantage by reducing internal infrastructure costs.
