### 🤖 Ancient Shipbuilding - Viking Wood Bending Techniques

This article details the historical methods employed by Vikings to shape the planks for ships like the Oseberg, focusing on the specialized wood preparation and bending process.

Key Points:

• Planks were split along the grain, preserving wood fiber integrity.

• Unbroken wood fibers maintained strength through curves.

• Heating oak with fire and steam softened the material.

• Softened wood allowed for precise, lasting bends.


🚀 Implementation:
1. Select Green Oak Logs: Choose fresh oak logs to ensure optimal workability.
2. Split Planks Along Grain: Divide the logs by splitting, not cutting, to maintain fiber continuity.
3. Apply Heat and Steam: Soften the oak planks over open fire using water for steam.
4. Shape the Planks: Bend the pliable wood into the desired forms for ship construction.
