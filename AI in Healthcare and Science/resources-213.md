### ✨ Claude AI Usage Reflection - Monthly Recap Feature

This article describes Claude AI's new usage reflection tool. It provides users with a monthly recap of their AI interaction patterns, offering insights into usage frequency and content.

Key Points:

• Users receive a monthly recap detailing Claude AI usage patterns.

• The feature offers options to set quiet hours and receive break nudges.

• It is available in beta across Free, Pro, and Max plans on web and desktop.

• The tool operates when memory is enabled, excluding incognito chats and health data.


🚀 Implementation:
1. Access Claude AI: Ensure you are logged into your Claude AI account.
2. Navigate to Settings: Find the "Settings" section within the user interface.
3. Locate Reflect Dashboard: Access your usage dashboard under the "Reflect" tab.

🔗 Resources:
• [Claude AI Settings](https://claude.ai/settings/reflect) - Access your Reflect dashboard

### 🤖 Decentralized Science (DeSci) - Architectural Vision

This article discusses the evolving vision for Decentralized Science (DeSci), emphasizing a shift from purely funding mechanisms to practical execution involving decentralized infrastructure and AI tools.

Key Points:

• DeSci challenges centralized control over scientific data and research processes.

• The vision promotes open datasets and broader community participation in healthspan research.

• DeSci is moving beyond funding into actual research execution.

• Future DeSci implementations combine decentralized infrastructure with clinical work.

• Agentic AI is an architectural component for advanced research tools within DeSci.
