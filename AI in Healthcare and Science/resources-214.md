### 🤖 Oncology - ALK+ NSCLC Treatment

Results from the ELEVATE phase 3 trial for adjuvant ensartinib in completely resected ALK+ Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC) have been published. The study reports disease-free survival rates and adverse event profiles.

Key Points:

• Adjuvant ensartinib in ALK+ NSCLC showed a 24-month DFS of 86.4% versus 53.5% for stage II–IIIB.

• The overall population saw 87.3% versus 57.2% 24-month DFS.

• The hazard ratio for DFS was 0.20 in both groups.

• Grade ≥3 adverse events occurred in 35.8% of patients, primarily rash.
