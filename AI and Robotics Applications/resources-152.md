### 🤖 Dark3R - Ultra-Low Light 3D Reconstruction

This article introduces Dark3R, a novel system capable of reconstructing camera poses and generating point clouds from images captured under extremely challenging ultra-low light conditions. It also enables the synthesis of new views based on this reconstructed 3D data.

Key Points:

• Recovers accurate camera poses even with minimal illumination.

• Generates dense 3D point clouds from noisy, low-light images.

• Enables realistic novel view synthesis from dark image inputs.

• Operates effectively at image signal-to-noise ratios below -4 dB.
