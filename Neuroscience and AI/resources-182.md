### 🤖 LLM Framework - Sleep Disorder Analysis

This article presents a novel multimodal, multi-agent Large Language Model (LLM) framework. Its primary application is to identify key drivers of sleep disorders.

Key Points:
• The framework employs a multimodal approach for data ingestion and analysis.

• It utilizes a multi-agent system architecture to process information.

• The core relies on Large Language Models to identify causal factors for sleep disorders.
