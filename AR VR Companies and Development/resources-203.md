### 🚀 Blender Add-on - Live Texture Painting

TXTRCast is a Blender add-on that enables real-time texture updates. Users can paint textures in external software like CSP or Photoshop and see changes instantly on their 3D models within Blender, streamlining the texturing process.

Key Points:

• Provides live texture painting synchronization from external editors to Blender.

• Supports texture painting software such as CSP and Photoshop.
