### 💡 VR Design - Embodiment vs. Immersion

This article discusses core concepts in VR design, differentiating between embodiment and immersion. It argues for the importance of embodiment in creating effective virtual experiences.

Key Points:

• Embodiment in VR refers to the feeling of owning a virtual body.

• Immersion describes the sense of being present within a virtual environment.

• The distinction between these concepts impacts effective VR experience design.
