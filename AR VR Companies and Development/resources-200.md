### 🚀 Animation Pipeline - iPhone Mocap to UE5

This article details a technical pipeline for converting iPhone motion capture data into animations for the Unreal Engine 5 skeleton. It describes how this process significantly reduces production time and costs for high-quality animation.

Key Points:

• Use an iPhone for recording motion capture data.

• Convert captured mocap data to a UE5 skeleton format.

• Reduce the time required for animation production.

• Achieve professional animation quality at a lower cost.


🚀 Implementation:
1. Record motion capture data using an iPhone.
2. Convert the recorded data to a UE5 skeleton.
3. Utilize Cascadeur 3D for animation processing.

🔗 Resources:

• [Cascadeur](https://x.com/cascadeur3d) - 3D animation software.

• [QuickMagic](https://x.com/QuickMagic_cn) - Tool for mocap conversion.
