Key Points:

• Direct connection to a screw terminal offers a secure and reliable ground.

• Avoid using crimped connectors if a direct wire connection is feasible.

• Ensure all electrical work adheres to local safety codes and standards.


🚀 Implementation:

3. Secure Connection: Form a hook with the bare wire and secure it directly under the grounding screw.
