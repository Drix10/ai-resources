### 🤖 AI - Agent Discovery Optimization (ADO)

This article describes the expected shift in customer interaction from direct website visits to engagements via AI agents. It explains the concept of Agent Discovery Optimization (ADO) as a method to ensure product visibility within agent interfaces.

Key Points:

• Future customer interactions will increasingly occur via AI agents instead of direct website visits.

• Agent Discovery Optimization (ADO) ensures a product appears directly within AI agent chat interfaces.

### 💡 WhatsApp - Disabling Text Animations

Recent WhatsApp updates have introduced new text animations. This guide provides instructions on how to disable these animations to revert to the application's previous display behavior.

Key Points:

• WhatsApp recently added new text animations.

• Users can disable these animations through the application's settings.

🚀 Implementation:
1. Open WhatsApp Settings.
2. Navigate to Chats.
3. Select Animations.
4. Turn off the toggle for "Messages."
