### 🤖 AI Model Performance - Human-in-the-Loop Strategy

This article addresses challenges in AI model decision-making that arise from insufficient context and proposes a Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) strategy to enhance model reliability. It outlines how incorporating human oversight can improve outcomes.

Key Points:

• Insufficient context causes AI models to generate inaccurate or irrelevant outputs.

• Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) designs improve AI model reliability.

• Frontloading key decisions with human input reduces errors and optimizes token use.


🚀 Implementation:
1. Identify Critical Decision Points: Determine where human judgment is most vital in the AI workflow.
2. Implement Human Review Gates: Design interfaces for human experts to provide input and make decisions.
3. Integrate Agent Execution: Allow the AI agent to proceed autonomously after human validation.
4. Monitor Performance Metrics: Track error rates and token usage to evaluate HITL effectiveness.
