### ✨ 4D Reconstruction - IronSight Prototype

Bilawal Sidhu developed IronSight, a 4D reconstruction prototype, using video input from two pairs of Ray-Ban Meta glasses. The system leverages Claude Fable 5 and was built rapidly without specialized hardware like LiDAR.

Key Points:
• IronSight performs 4D reconstruction from consumer-grade glasses video.

• The prototype uses Claude Fable 5 for processing.

• It avoids the need for LiDAR or studio equipment.

• The project was completed quickly as a weekend prototype.
