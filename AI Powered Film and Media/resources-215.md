### 🤖 Model Interpretability - Concept Erasure

MANCE is a method designed to remove specific concepts from model representations while preserving other relevant information. It achieves this by constraining edits to the natural data manifold.

Key Points:

• It preserves other data during concept erasure operations.

• The method operates by limiting edits to the data manifold.
