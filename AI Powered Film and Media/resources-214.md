### 🚀 Maestro - Local AI Music Video Generator

Maestro is a local-first application designed to automate music video and short film production. It handles content creation from concept to final edit without requiring cloud services or subscriptions.

Key Points:

• Maestro generates original songs, shot plans, and screenplays.

• It automatically creates and edits video clips based on the planned content.

• The system operates entirely locally, avoiding cloud dependencies.

• The tool is free and offers a one-click installation process.

🚀 Implementation:
1. Describe your desired music video or short film concept to Maestro.
2. The tool generates an original song, detailed shot list, and screenplay based on your input.
3. Maestro proceeds to generate and edit video clips, assembling the final production.
4. Install the application via Pinokio for a streamlined setup process.
