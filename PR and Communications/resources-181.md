### 🤖 AI Adoption - Use Case Trends

This article examines recent trends in artificial intelligence adoption, based on an analysis of over 12,000 real-world AI use cases. It highlights shifts in how people utilize AI tools.

Key Points:

• AI is increasingly used for practical assistance and entertainment.

• Applications related to self-improvement, such as life organization or finding purpose, have decreased.

• Research indicates a change in AI's role for users.
