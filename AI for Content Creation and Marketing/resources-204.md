### 💡 MMARA - Custom AI for Specialized Domains

MMARA's founders opted to build a custom AI solution instead of integrating existing large language models. This decision prioritizes user trust and domain-specific accuracy, particularly in sensitive areas like women's health.

Key Points:

• Generic LLMs can exhibit performance limitations in specialized application domains.

• Developing custom AI allows for tailored accuracy in specific use cases.

• Prioritizing user trust can drive investments in proprietary model development.

• A custom AI development path, while slower, can yield better long-term reliability.
