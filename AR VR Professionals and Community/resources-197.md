### ✨ Maya Shaders - Custom Eye Real-Time Rendering

This article describes a custom real-time eye shader developed for Maya's viewport. It highlights the shader's capabilities and design principles. The shader aims to provide extensive customization for character artists.

Key Points:
• The shader operates in real-time within the Maya viewport.

• It utilizes a single shader and a single geometry for rendering.

• Multiple presets and extensive customization options are available.
