### 💡 Meta Devices - Account Binding Issues

This article describes an observed issue where new or reset Meta Quest and Ray-Ban devices fail to properly bind to Meta accounts. This can lead to various login and feature access problems for users.

Key Points:
• New or recently reset Quest and Ray-Ban devices exhibit binding failures.

• Device serial numbers do not properly associate with Meta accounts.

• This issue can result in login problems for shared device surfaces.

• Quest devices may lose the ability to toggle specific Public Test Channel (PTC) features.
